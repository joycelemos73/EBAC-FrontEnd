About the festival

Get ready to ignite your passion for metal music at the Hellfire Festival - a three-day extravaganza featuring over 25 of the hottest metal acts in the world. With a crowd of over 300,000 people, this festival is a must-attend event for any metalhead looking to experience the ultimate in live music. From headbanging riffs to mind-blowing solos, the Hellfire Festival is the ultimate celebration of all things metal. Join us for an unforgettable weekend of pure, unbridled energy and metal madness!
